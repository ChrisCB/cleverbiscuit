Piggybanker

Piggybanker is an easy to use comparison website helping consumers make financially savvy decisions.

We aim to help people who are put off by financial jargon to still make informed decisions about their finances.

Visit www.piggybanker.it, the interest calculator microsite and the Facebook application.

Search Latte

Search Latte allows you to build Google searches in any combination of Google supported top level domain, country and language.

Quickly check international non-personalised SERPs, also supports synonyms and 'allintitle' commands.

Visit searchlatte.com.

RecruitEm

RecruitEm let's you easily use Google to search profiles on LinkedIn, Google+, GitHub, Twitter, Stack Overflow and more

More than 100 results, out of network connections, search by education, see full profiles. All for free!

Visit recruitin.net.

Not sure what looks good on you? Want to learn how to dress well?

Visit Mustard Threads.
